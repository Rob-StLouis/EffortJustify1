Round 2: Social Version

In the previous round, you got some paintings and skipped others. We have randomly selected a previous participant and will give you 9 of their skipped paintings Your goal is to collect all the paintings that they skipped and then try to predict their value. The participant who is most accurate at guessing the paintings value will win $10.00.

Round 2: Non Social: Version

Your goal is to collect each painting and then estimate its value. The participant who is most accurate at guessing the paintings value will win $10.00.
